Cowin Pinger

Scroll down to download the app

Using this App, ypu can set an automatic alert system for your preferred district and age bracket

The script checks the Cowin API every 1 minute

If the notifications are too frequent, you can always go and delete the scheduled notifications

NOTE: This app works like a music player. If you kill the app, the notifications will stop. just push the app into the background (i.e switch to another app or the homescreen) and the notifications will continue.

Please suggest a better name, or raise any issue or reccomendation to the developer via email at vaxslots.dev@gmail.com

Currently only available for android, IOS version coming soon!

Usage

On startup, you will be greeted with this homepage

Kindly read through the information provided for best user experience

After clicking continue, you will be directed to the SCHEDULE NOTIFICATIONS page

Here you can select your preferred state, district and age bracket for which you want notifications

You can set and delete multiple notification jobs

For example, you can select your state, district and age bracket.

When you're done selecting, click on the ADD button to schedule notifications

NOTE: Please do not click on DONE directly after making your selection. First click on ADD, then click on DONE to go to the NOTIFICATIONS JOB page

Once you click ADD you will be intimated by an alert that the job has been scheduled

If the job is already scheduled, (i.e. if the same selection has been previously added) you will be intimated about duplicate jobs too

After clicking DONE on the previous page, you will be directed to the NOTIFICATIONS LIST page

Here you will be able to see a list of all your scheduled jobs

To delete a job, click on the icon button next to the job given in the list.

To add more jobs, you can click the icon button to go back to the SCHEDULE NOTIFICATIONS page
